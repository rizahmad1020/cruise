Aloha! Welcome to Your Dream Hawaiian Vacation

Experience the magic of Hawaii like never before aboard our luxury cruise ships. Our Hawaiian island-hopping adventure takes you to the most breathtaking destinations across the archipelago, combining the comfort of a floating resort with the thrill of discovering multiple islands.

From the dramatic cliffs of the Napali Coast to the fiery volcanoes of the Big Island, each day brings new wonders. Enjoy authentic luaus, world-class snorkeling, and the legendary Hawaiian hospitality as you sail from one paradise to another.

Your Hawaiian Cruise Highlights

Private Island Beaches

Exclusive access to pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

Authentic Cultural Experiences

Participate in traditional hula lessons, lei-making workshops, and authentic Hawaiian luaus.

Adventure Excursions

From volcano hikes to helicopter tours and submarine adventures, thrill-seekers will love our excursions.

Luxury Amenities

Enjoy spa treatments with local ingredients, gourmet Hawaiian cuisine, and premium staterooms with ocean views.

7-Day Island-Hopping Itinerary

Day 1: Oahu

Embark in Honolulu with a welcome lei ceremony. Explore Waikiki Beach or visit Pearl Harbor before our sunset departure.

Day 2-3: Maui

Two days to explore the Road to Hana, HaleakalƒÅ sunrise, and Lahaina's historic whaling village. Optional luau experience.

Day 4: Big Island

Discover Hawaii Volcanoes National Park, black sand beaches, and Kona coffee plantations.

Day 5-6: Kauai

Marvel at the Napali Coast, Waimea Canyon, and take a river cruise to Fern Grotto. Helicopter tours available.

Day 7: Return to Oahu

Disembark with memories to last a lifetime, or extend your stay to explore more of Oahu at your leisure.
