CruiseSphere Cruise Information

Onboard Experiences

CruiseSphere Cruises offers a variety of dining options including 5 specialty restaurants, 3 main dining rooms, buffet options, and 24-hour room service. Our culinary team prepares international cuisine with regional specialties.

Enjoy Broadway-style shows at our theater, live music performances, comedy clubs, cinema, casino, and various themed parties. We also offer enrichment programs including cooking classes and art workshops.

Destinations

Our cruises visit spectacular destinations across the Red Sea, Mediterranean, and American mexican including Jeddah, Sharm El Sheikh, Safaga, Dubai, Barcelona, and Santorini.

We offer both included and optional shore excursions. Each port has at least one complimentary activity, with additional premium excursions available for booking.

Explore CruiseSphere

CruiseSphere combines American hospitality with international cruise standards, offering unique cultural experiences, halal dining options, prayer facilities, and family-friendly entertainment tailored for our guests.

We offer Interior, Oceanview, Balcony, and Suite categories with various subcategories including Family Cabins and Accessible Cabins. All feature modern American-inspired decor.

Policies

Cancellations made 90+ days before sailing receive full refund. 50% refund for 60-89 days, 25% for 30-59 days. No refunds within 30 days. Travel insurance is recommended.

We follow international health standards with enhanced cleaning, medical facilities onboard, and trained staff. Vaccination requirements vary by itinerary and are updated regularly.
